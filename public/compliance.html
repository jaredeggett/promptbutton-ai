The First Human-Powered AI Device

You already power most of the things that matter. Your AI is just next.

Coffee machines don't run themselves. Cars need a driver. The economy needs you awake by 9. PromptButton adds one more: your AI.

Inside the button is a spring-loaded electromagnetic generator. When you press down, that mechanical force is converted into a burst of electrical energy, just enough to broadcast a single BLE 5.0 packet. Your code, running on any device within 30 meters, picks it up and takes over.

The technology is proven. The same mechanism has powered millions of wireless light switches in commercial buildings for over 20 years. What's new is where the energy goes. Until now, a press turned on a light. Now, a press starts an AI.

It is a small change. A very small change. But someone has to go first.
